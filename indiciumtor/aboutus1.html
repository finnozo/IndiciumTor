Indicium Tor

About us The website "Indicium Tor" is designed and developed by "TechnoCrats". As with the advancement in field of Science and Technology, all the resources are available on the finger tips of people. So we " The TechnoCrats " have crafted a website which will cater all the basic requirement of Students that is expected by Student from an Educational Portal. This website is designed only for Students, Alumni's and Head of Department of National Institute Of Technology ,Raipur.

What Indicium Tor provides..? Student with a valid Login and Password can enter the website and make use of all the resources available. Student section allows students to view/download the resources (E-books, Magazines,Videos) or they can even upload some if they have any. Students can easily find the resource of their need just by providing the Stream/Branch and semester. Students are priviliged to put their queries/complaints/suggestion to their respective Head anonymously and will b replied back by Head for the same. In Discussion Section ,if any student having any query can post it to this section and will b replied back on it by other students.Query can be related to any topic/issue. Every branch has its own discussion section that allows students of only that branch to reply in it.

What separates us from others..?

  1. As most of the Educational websites offers students pools of resources, but fails to provide a common platform where users can put their queries and get the answers from other users, but we at "Indicium Tor" provides you with this functionality through Discussion Section.
  2. It isint easy for students to directly communicate with their Head of Department on some issue,so they can post their queries to their Head , and they are kept anonymous from head.
